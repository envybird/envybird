About

Vervain | 18 | she/her | Chinese-Indonesian

Hello! I'm Envybird (or a more casual name; Vervain), I'm currently an IT student and the #1 instant noodle fan. I'm the creator of several oc projects, but for now the most notable one is a slice-of-life webcomic named Project Madelung. This is my personal site in which I'll post stuff like daily life shenanigans, non-canon oc stuff, self-indulgent knick-knacks, and more.

My main interests are chemistry and Homestuck, others are Hetalia, Project Sekai, Hoyoverse, Houseki no Kuni, Cells at Work!, The Battle Cats, Neko Atsume, and more.

Favorite characters: John Egbert, Jade Harley, Mizuki Akiyama, Tino Väinämöinen, Sunday, Furina
